# Talk Tracks

These are reference examples.

Do not repeat them word for word.

## Principle

Mention problems as the hook, then move into the value prop.

## Sample live talk track, `~90s`

1. Opener:
   - `Hi {name}, it's {rep} with Openmart, quick 30 seconds?`
2. Hook:
   - `The reason for my call is that most sales leaders have told me that incomplete and dirty data is killing rep productivity.`
3. Value:
   - `Openmart auto-enriches every account and pushes phone-verified contacts into Salesforce in 60 seconds.`
4. Discovery:
   - `How much time did your team spend calling unqualified leads this week?`
5. Proof:
   - `DoorDash's sales team saved 10 analyst hours a week and hit 95% contact coverage in 30 days.`
6. CTA:
   - `Worth 15 minutes tomorrow at 10 or 2 PT to see if we could replicate that?`
7. Close phrase:
   - `Great, I'll send the calendar invite now. Appreciate the candor.`

## Coaching examples

### Example 1

Positives:

- prospect is engaged
- prospect gives discovery material and time

Negatives:

- SDR fails to book the meeting despite little pushback

Takeaway:

- this should have led to a demo
- the leak is usually in the close, not the discovery

### Example 2

Positives:

- SDR responds to pushback well
- SDR keeps the prospect engaged
- SDR books the meeting

Negatives:

- prospect is likely unqualified

Takeaway:

- good framework execution can still happen on a weak-fit call
- keep qualification tight so calendar time is not wasted

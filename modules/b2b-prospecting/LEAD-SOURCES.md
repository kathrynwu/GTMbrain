# B2B Prospecting Lead Sources

Use this note when the question is where a B2B or software-company list should
come from.

## Preferred rule in this repo

- `Apollo` is the default starting point for software-company prospecting
- add another database only when it clearly improves contact coverage for the
  exact market you need
- avoid purchased lists unless quality is already proven

## Why Apollo-first

In this repo, Apollo is the default B2B source because:

- it fits software-company prospecting better than local-business sourcing tools
- the company and title filters are usually enough to stand up a first list
- it is the cleanest default when the buyer is a company operator, director, or
  VP

## When to add another source

Only add another source when there is a real reason:

- better enterprise coverage
- better regional coverage
- better contact coverage for the same segment

Do not turn source choice into a giant tool-comparison project.

## Best-practice rules

- choose the source based on the segment, not brand popularity
- preserve company identity at merge time
- preserve buyer title and trigger context
- verify contactability before launch
- treat stale data as a list-quality problem

## What not to do

- do not buy a huge list and hope scale fixes bad targeting
- do not lead with junior titles just because they reply more
- do not skip verification because the source says the row is verified

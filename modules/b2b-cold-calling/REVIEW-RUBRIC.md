# B2B Cold Call Review Rubric

Use this rubric to review a cold call script, live call, or rep recording.

Score each section:

- `2` strong
- `1` partial
- `0` weak or missing

## 1. Credibility

Check:

- did they sound confident from the first hello
- did they state who they are and why they are calling immediately
- did they avoid awkward pauses, stuttering, and filler words
- did the opener sound polished and direct

## 2. Value

Check:

- did they show they understand the prospect's business
- did they ask short questions to uncover pain
- did they listen and respond to what they heard
- did they explain why the offer is better than the status quo

## 3. Close

Check:

- did they define the next step clearly
- did they ask for a concrete day and time
- did they send the invite promptly
- did they try to get acceptance while still on the phone

## Recommended score interpretation

- `5-6`: usable, keep refining
- `3-4`: partial, but likely leaking meetings
- `0-2`: rewrite the opener, value section, and close
